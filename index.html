Southern Arizona Performing Arts Company (SAPAC) is a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization of unified local artists committed to bringing high-quality theatrical performance to the Tucson area. We believe in the power theater has to entertain, edify, and connect audience members and performers alike. SAPAC creates opportunity for local artists in all aspects of theater to learn and grow in a professional atmosphere while fostering a positive effect on the community, building empathy and connections throughout Tucson.

Inaugural Broadway Gala 2019

Join us for a evening of cocktails, hors d'oeuvres, and a dozen of Tucson's best singers and entertainers as we kick-off the beginning of Southern Arizona Performing Arts Company. This event will feature the music of Broadway from classics to modern favorites. Attendees will also get a preview of SAPAC's first season and have an opportunity to help build Tucson's newest musical theater company.

Performances from Armen Dirtadian, Crystal Stark, Katherine Byrnes, Chach Snook, Tyler Wright, Juan Aguirre, Jodi Darling, Erin Anderson, Matthew Holter, Kit Runge, Khris Dodge, and Liz Cracchiolo.

  • 6pm Cocktail Hour with Silent Auction
  • 7pm Performance

SAPAC is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization and all proceeds from this event will be used to develop this new production company.
