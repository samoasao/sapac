Southern Arizona Performing Arts Company (SAPAC) is a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization of unified local artists committed to bringing high-quality theatrical performance to the Tucson area. We believe in the power theater has to entertain, edify, and connect audience members and performers alike. SAPAC creates opportunity for local artists in all aspects of theater to learn and grow in a professional atmosphere while fostering a positive effect on the community, building empathy and connections throughout Tucson.

Gutenberg! The Musical!

by Anthony King and Scott Brown

Unscrewed Theater

August 15-25, 2019

In this two-man musical spoof, a pair of aspiring playwrights perform a backers’ audition for their new project - a big, splashy musical about printing press inventor Johann Gutenberg. With an unending supply of enthusiasm, Bud and Doug sing all the songs and play all the parts in their historical epic, with the hope that one of the producers in attendance will give them a Broadway contract – fulfilling their ill-advised dreams.

SAPAC is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization and all proceeds from this event will be used to develop this new production company.

by Jeff Bowen and Hunter Bell

Cabaret Theater, Temple of Music and Art

September 13-22, 2019

Chronicling the lives of Jeff and Hunter, two struggling writers, hurriedly writing a musical to submit to a theatre festival. With the deadline looming, the pair enlists the help of their friends Susan, Heidi and Larry on the piano, and make a pact to write up until the deadline and submit what they have in the hopes that it may change their lives. Eventually, Jeff suggests they simply write about what to write about. In the span of 90 minutes they write and perform their show at the festival, learning lessons about themselves along the way. It is metatheatre at its most hilarious!
